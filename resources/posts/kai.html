Hueningkai (휴닝카이) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and producer under Big Hit Music. He is a member of the boy group TOMORROW X TOGETHER.
On January 15, 2019, Hueningkai was introduced as the third member of TOMORROW X TOGETHER with the release of his teaser video on YouTube.
The group debuted on March 4, 2019 with their first mini album The Dream Chapter: STAR.
